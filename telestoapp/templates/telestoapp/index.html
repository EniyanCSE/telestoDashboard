    Our Partners

    BLADE ENERGY PARTNERS

    Blade is an independent consulting group that focuses on resolving the challenges of complex projects engineering and delivery of complex projects for upstream oil and gas industry across the globe. Primarily in solving complex subsurface, drilling, completion, production material, and pipeline integrity problems using their leading-edge expertise. Blade has successfully delivered projects working with industry leaders on a variety of engineering, research, and development projects. In addition, they provide technical training primarily focused on drilling and completions.

    Visit Website

    CLONESKILLS

    CloneSkills is our partner in developing Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Big Data Analytics (BDA) solutions for the upstream oil and gas industry. The team of CloneSkills consists of alumni’s of Harvard and Stanford along with leading management consultants from KPMG, Deloitte, and Ernst & Young. This has helped them to be industry leaders in Enterprise Artificial Intelligence and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT). Primarily for providing prebuild AI platform with IIoT and big data capabilities for enterprise that helps companies in quickly implementing machine learning, and deep learning models for realization of measurable business values
